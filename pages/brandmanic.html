Brandmanic V3

Developing and improving SaaS for influencer marketing

This project consisted of the evolution of a SaaS platform for influencer marketing, starting from a previous version (v2). My role was to transform and optimize the user experience (UX/UI), adapting the platform to new market needs and aligning it with current design trends. Through a user-centered approach, I redefined the information architecture, improved usability, and implemented a more modern and intuitive visual design.

Design of the new interface

The development of the new interface went through a series of key stages before arriving at the final result. It started with a market study, analyzing similar platforms to identify common patterns and elements that would enhance our SaaS.

With the information gathered, the first initial wireframes were designed, capturing a preliminary proposal for the interface. These were presented in meetings with the development and marketing teams to gather opinions and adjust the proposal based on the feedback received. Subsequently, a second iteration of wireframes was developed, refining the structure and functionalities.

Next, the design was transferred to Figma, where the first visual versions of the interface were created. Once this phase was completed, a product test was carried out with the marketing team, who acted as main testers.

After multiple rounds of testing and adjustments based on feedback, the design was implemented in code, maintaining constant communication with the team to evaluate the evolution and record changes and improvements throughout the process.
